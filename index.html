ABOUT US

Established in 2003 SPD Memorial School is an UP Board English medium co-educational School It is the beginning of a lifelong adventure known as learning. The School nurtures a spirit that fosters children's natural inquisitiveness and delight in learning.


S.P.D Memorial School is committed to creating and maintaining a trusting and caring environment where teaching and learning are exciting and students embark on a life long journey of learning.
All aspects of the school's organisation, curricular, and co-curricular activities are child centred and designed to accommodate individual learning styles so that all may experience success.
The mission of S.P.D memorial School is to help students acquire the knowledge, skills and attitudes necessary to become happy, healthy, well adjusted, productive citizens of the world.

Principal's Message

❝

The illiterates of 21st century will not be those who cannot read and write,but those who cannot learn, unlearn and relearn.

- Alvin Tuffer

There are a few key characteristics that distinguish good schools from the rest. Effective & committed management team, well qualified, experienced teaching & supporting staff, an engagement model between every child’s home and school that partners for the comprehensive growth of the child, extensive academic and extra-curricular programs are certainly the key factors.

Further, I believe, two components distinguish the excellent from those that are average. First, the willingness & flexibility to adapt to the changes in the society and second, appreciating the fact that the needs of the students always come first.

At Jnana Vijnana Vidyapeetha (J.V.V.P) we believe that our sole endeavor is to be on the path to excellence and put the best interests of our students above all. We encourage a system of constant learning of high values, humility and integrity. I encourage all parents to unite with us to make this institution a platform for learning, action, creativity and personal growth for their children. Join us in our efforts to transform your children into enlightened citizens and the pillars of our future society.
